TED Mission Funding Platform — Prototype

A clickable mock-up (not a live system) showing how unions and missions across the Trans-European Division could submit, review, fund and report mission projects in one place.

  • Runs locally in the browser — nothing is sent online or stored on a server.
  • Demo data is reset on page refresh.
  • Use the “Signed in as" switch (top right) — each role sees only its own applications.

Unions / fields (abbreviations only): NUC = Norwegian UC · PUC = Polish UC · GM = Greek Mission · AUC = Adriatic UC
